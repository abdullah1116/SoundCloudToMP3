To download, you do not need to go through plenty of steps. All you have to do is copy and paste. You can either use the keyboard or mouse to perform this task. However, we allow an easy search of tracks by entering the artist's name or song without the need of adding any URL.

Let us go through a step-by-step guide to get offline music for all ages and moods.

Step One

  • Search the artist, song, and playlist of your choice
  • Or open Soundcloud.com
  • Copy the URL of the selected songs or playlist
  • Open SoundcloudtoMP3.live
  • Paste the link in the search bar at the top of the page
  • Enter search 

Step Two

Once you have entered the search, the required track or playlist will appear in the same window. You can either play or download it as per your choice.

For download

  • Click on the drop-down arrow
  • It will lead you to a new tab
  • Check the file size and click on the download button

Since we have promised you a user-friendly application, the previous page will have the track as it is. So that if you want to go back and choose another song to download from the playlist, you can do it in one go.

If you have a particular song in your mind, write that in the search bar right away and get maximum results immediately.

How to Copy URL/Link

Either you are using SoundCloud downloader chrome, SoundCloud downloader android, Soundcloud downloader mac, or any other device there are options to copy.

For PC

It is easier to copy the link from the bar at the top of the page. You can copy the link from there and paste it here at the search bar.

For Mobile

For mobile users, I recommend using the share option placed at the bottom of each track. From there, you can copy the link and paste it here in the search bar.
